About

Draw a hieroglyph and the app matches it against the 769 canonical Gardiner signs using a neural encoder that runs locally in your browser — no drawing ever leaves your device. After the first visit it works offline.

Recognizer trained on the Hand-drawn Hieroglyph Dataset (Aneesh et al., IC4 2024); canonical glyphs and sign data from the ArchaeoHack starter pack. Match quality is best for complete, single-sign drawings.
